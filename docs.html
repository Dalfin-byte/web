How to setup?

WINDOWS: Press Win + R to open the Run dialog. Type %APPDATA%\.minecraft and press Enter. In the .minecraft folder, locate or create a folder named resourcepacks. Place your .zip texture/resource pack file into this folder. Launch Minecraft, go to Options > Resource Packs, and select your pack.


MACOS: Open Finder. Press Cmd + Shift + G to open the "Go to Folder" dialog. Enter ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft and press Enter. Inside the minecraft folder, find or create a resourcepacks folder. Place your .zip texture/resource pack file here. Open Minecraft, navigate to Options > Resource Packs, and choose your pack.


LINUX: Open your home directory. Press Ctrl + H to show hidden files. Navigate to .minecraft. Inside .minecraft, find or create a resourcepacks folder. Place your .zip texture/resource pack file into this folder. Launch Minecraft, go to Options > Resource Packs, and select your pack.
